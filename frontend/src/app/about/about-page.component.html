What is Lexiq?

Lexiq is an interactive language learning app that takes you from zero to conversational Italian. Built with a focus on Bulgarian speakers, every lesson, exercise type, and feedback mechanism is designed to match how Bulgarian learners think and progress.

Features

📚

Interactive Lessons

Structured courses guide you through Italian from beginner to advanced. Each lesson unlocks the next, keeping you on a clear learning path.

✏️

Diverse Exercises

Fill-in-the-blank, listening comprehension, true/false, image choice, and audio matching — every exercise type reinforces a different skill.

❤️

Hearts System

You start each session with 5 hearts. Wrong answers cost a heart — pushing you to think before you answer. Hearts refill over time.

⭐

XP & Leveling

Earn XP for every correct answer. Your level grows with your total points, giving a tangible measure of how far you've come.

🔥

Daily Streaks

Keep your streak alive by practicing every day. Consistency is the number one predictor of language learning success.

🏆

Leaderboard

Compete with other learners on daily, weekly, and all-time rankings. A little friendly competition goes a long way.
