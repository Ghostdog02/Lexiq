Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2025

Introduction

Lexiq is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your information. It applies to all users of relexiq.com and any associated applications. By using Lexiq, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google's OAuth service. We store your name and email to identify your account and communicate with you. We do not store your Google password.

Usage Data

We record your learning activity — including lessons completed, exercises attempted, answers submitted, XP earned, streaks, and heart counts. This data drives your progress tracking, leaderboard rankings, and personalised experience.

Cookies

We use a single HttpOnly, Secure session cookie (AuthToken) to keep you logged in. This cookie is strictly necessary for authentication and is not used for advertising or cross-site tracking. We do not use third-party analytics cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide and improve the Lexiq service: to authenticate you, display your progress, calculate XP and level, maintain your streak, show leaderboard rankings, and send transactional emails if you contact support. We may also use aggregated, anonymised data to understand how learners use the platform and to guide future content development.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising networks. Your data is shared only in the following limited cases: with Google (for OAuth sign-in, governed by their privacy policy); with our hosting provider (Hetzner, solely for infrastructure); or if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Lexiq and its users.

Data Retention

We retain your account data and learning records for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance reasons. Anonymised aggregate statistics may be retained indefinitely.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including HTTPS-only communication, HttpOnly cookies, and regular security reviews. No system is perfectly secure; if you suspect your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at support@relexiq.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data; to object to or restrict certain processing; and to receive a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@relexiq.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please reach us at support@relexiq.com. We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days.
